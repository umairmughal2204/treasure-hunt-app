Getting Started

Welcome to the Treasure Hunt game! To begin playing:

  • 🧍 Enter your name in the input field.
  • 🗺️ Select a treasure hunt from the dropdown.
  • 🚀 Click Start to begin your adventure.

Answering Questions

  • ❓ Read the question carefully.
  • 💬 Enter your answer or choose the correct option.
  • ✅ Press Submit to send your answer.
  • ⏭️ Use Skip if you're stuck — but remember, skips may cost points!

Location Access

This game may ask for your location to unlock certain questions. Please ensure:

  • 📍 Location access is enabled in your browser.
  • 🔐 You allow permissions when prompted.

Troubleshooting

  • 🔄 Refresh the page if a question doesn't load.
  • 📡 Check your internet connection.
  • 💡 Try using Chrome or Firefox for the best experience.
